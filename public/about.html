About Me

Hello, I'm Rohan Ekbote, a third-year Information Technology (IT) engineering student at D.Y Patil College of Engineering, Akurdi. I'm a passionate web developer, and I'd like to tell you a bit about myself and why I love web development

So, what is web development? Well, it's like building the digital homes and stores you visit on the internet. Think of it as constructing a website, just like an architect designs and builds a house. Web developers like me work on creating the structure, design, and functionality of these websites, making them look good and work smoothly for people like you who use them.

My passion for web development is not just about coding and designing websites; it's also about making the online world a better place, one website at a time. I enjoy the feeling of accomplishment when I see my work live on the internet, accessible to people all around the world. In a nutshell, I'm Rohan, a web developer, and I'm passionate about building the digital spaces where people can connect, learn, shop, and share their ideas. It's like being a digital architect and artist, and I absolutely love what I do!
